MP5

The MP5A4 is a submachine gun which shoots Pistol Bullets and holds 30 rounds of ammunition. It is the only military grade weapon in Rust that can be crafted.

Satchel Charge

The Satchel Charge is an unreliable raiding tool that can be used to destroy player-made buildings for the purpose of entering other players bases and looting them.

Pumpkin

The Pumpkin is a food item that can be found growing at riversides and planted around the world by players. When eaten, a pumpkin restores 10 health, 100 hunger, and 15 thirst.

Rust Lore

Here is where it all begins. Naked and afraid. Rust is brutal and unforgiving, no tutorial, no directions, just a world to explore and survive in. You will have to find food, water, and shelter. When you spawn in Rust for the first time, you find yourself on a beach with nothing but a rock and a torch.

The objective of Rust is to survive in the wilderness using gathered or stolen materials. Players must successfully manage their hunger, thirst, and health, or risk dying. Despite the presence of hostile animals such as bears and wolves, the primary threat to the player is other players due to the game being solely multiplayer. Trust no one.
